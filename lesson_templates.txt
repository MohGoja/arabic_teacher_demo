Lesson Templates Summary
------------------------

1. Slide
   - Each lesson consists of multiple slides.
   - Each slide has a title, slide_number, and blocks.

2. Block Types
   - text: Simple text content.
   - heading: Section or topic heading.
   - footnote: Additional notes or clarifications.
   - graph: Visual representation of relationships (nodes/roots).
   - animation: Reference to an animation asset (with name/type).
   - quiz: Multiple-choice questions with options and correct answers.
   - multiStepQuiz: Multi-step quiz, often with highlighted text segments.
   - example: List of example words or phrases.
   - info: Q&A style information blocks.

3. Quiz Templates
   - Standard quiz: Question, highlighted word, options, correct answer.
   - Multi-step quiz: Text with highlighted segments, each with options.

4. Graph Templates
   - Root node with child nodes, sometimes nested.

5. Animation Templates
   - Animation name and type (large/small).

6. Info/Q&A Templates
   - Question and answer segments, sometimes with style info.

7. Example Templates
   - List of example words or sentences.

------------------------
For each lesson, slides use combinations of these block types to present content, exercises, and visual aids.
